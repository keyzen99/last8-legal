# Privacy Policy (BeBeat.)

This Privacy Policy applies to **BeBeat. – Rhythm for Practice** (hereinafter "the App").

## Information We Collect
The App does not collect any personal information.
All data, including practice time records, is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

## Third-Party Sharing
The App does not share data with any third parties.

## Analytics
The App does not use any analytics tools.
If introduced in future versions, it will be opt-in, and this policy will be updated accordingly.

## Contact
If you have questions, please contact us at:

- Email: apps@kentaroyamamoto.com

## Changes
This policy may be updated without prior notice.
Changes become effective upon posting on this page.
